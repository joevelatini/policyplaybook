MSP Licensing Policy üìÑ Source Doc

Requirement: Only licensed, certified MSPs may manage customer accounts.

Compliant Options:

  1. Customer's MSP is enrolled in official MSP program
  2. Customer manages their own account directly
  3. Customer transitions to a licensed MSP

Non-Compliance Consequences:

  • Account suspension pending remediation
  • Account termination for breach

Decision Authority:

  • Matt Finlayson owns termination decisions
  • 50+ seat accounts: Matt consults with Eric and Gabriela
Talking Point:
"Unlicensed MSPs lack the certification and oversight needed to prevent configuration errors. Moving forward, all MSPs must be licensed. You can manage your account directly or transition to a licensed MSP partner."
